Event Details

Welcome to the First season of SEEK Asia Hackathon. This is an event that everyone are welcome to join! All individual with unparalleled passion come together to engage and execute your creative idea in this Hackathon.

In this First season of SEEK Asia Hackathon we open up to the participant to build anything... yes ANYTHING... Is an 'Open Theme' for participant to hack anything you can think of.

But if you would like to win, here are some tips for you. Judges would usually like to see projects that are innovative, and have the potential to improve or influence people’s lives. Try to build a hack that people can use, enjoy, and potentially scale. Most importantly, have fun with it and try to finish in time. We know you can only do so much in 12 hours, so it’s ok if your project is not perfect (yet).

FAQ

What exactly is a Hackathon?

In short, Hackathon is a one day event where people come together to solve problems.

Participants will form groups of about 3-6 individuals, take out their laptops, and dive into the problems together as a team.

Why are we doing the Hackathon?
  • Provide an opportudisc for us all to learn something new together.
  • Provide a space and a time for us to make headway on problems we are interested in.
Is there a theme for this Hackathon?

Since this is our first SEEK Asia Hackathon, we want to keep the theme open. As long as the work is in some way related to SEEK Asia, all ideas are welcome!

What are the expected outcomes?

Don’t code? No worries, any sort of presentation will be accepted. e.g. documents, demo, prototype, research, etc. As long as you can find an interesting way to present your work!

Who can join the Hackthon?

Remember, Hackathon is creative problem solving and It doesn’t necessarily have to be all about technology. As Daniel mentioned, regardless of which role, team or department you are from you will be able to contribute to a Hack project. Work together to make your ideas a reality and a chance to innovate for SEEK Asia!

How do I sign up?
  • Phase 1 - Ideas submission (OPEN NOW! Closing date: 6PM on November 6th.)

    Got an idea you’d like to work on? Submit your idea here. Don’t worry if you don’t have an idea, you can participate the pitch in phase 2 and join a team instead.

  • Phase 2 - Pitches (November 10th. Time: TBC)

    Pitches are open to public. Idea owners will pitch the idea to the audience and tell them why they should join your team.

  • Phase 3 - Form teams (November 10th - November 24th)

    An online spreadsheet will be available for you to sign up to look at ideas and sign up the idea you are interested to work on. Please note minimum 3 people are required to form a team and work on the idea and each team should have no more than 6 people.

More questions?

We will follow up with food, facility, detail agenda and judging criteria soon. In the meantime, please feel free to reach out to regional committee for questions. We hope to see you all at the event!
